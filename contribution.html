Contributions

  • Theory of Relativity

    This theory revolutionized the understanding of space, time, and motion. It introduced the concept that the laws of physics are the same for all non-accelerating observers, and the speed of light in a vacuum is the same no matter the speed at which an observer travels.

  • Photoelectric Effect

    Einstein proposed that light is made up of particles called photons, and each photon carries a certain amount of energy. This work won him the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921.
